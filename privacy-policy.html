Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

MyPic2PDF (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the MyPic2PDF – Fast & Simple Image to PDF mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

MyPic2PDF does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No account registration is required
  • No name, phone number, or address is collected
  • No images or PDFs are uploaded to any server

All image processing and PDF creation happens locally on your device.

2. Images & Files

  • Images selected from Gallery or Camera remain on your device
  • Generated PDF files are stored locally on your phone
  • We do not access, back up, or share your files

3. Notifications

MyPic2PDF may show basic app notifications.

  • Notifications can be enabled or disabled anytime in Settings
  • No personal data is used for notifications

4. Internet Usage

The app works fully offline.

Internet access is only required if you choose to:

  • Open the Privacy Policy link
  • Send feedback via email

5. AI-Generated Graphics

Some app graphics (such as icons, splash images, or illustrations) may be AI-generated.

These visuals are used for design purposes only and do not collect or process any user data.

6. Third-Party Services

MyPic2PDF does not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools.

The app may use system features such as:

  • Email apps (for feedback)
  • File sharing apps (when you choose to share a PDF)

7. Children’s Privacy

MyPic2PDF is safe for all age groups. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
